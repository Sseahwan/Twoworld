The World Wide Web (abbrereviated WWW or Web) is an information space where documents and Web resources are identified by Uniform Resource Locators (URLs), inrerlinked by hypertext links, and accessible via the Internet.

[1] Engilsh scientist Tim Berners-Lee invented the World Wide Web in 1989. He wrote the first web browser in 1990 while emplpyed at CERN in Switzerland.
